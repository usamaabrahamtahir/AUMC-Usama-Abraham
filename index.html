Air University Multan Campus

Air University Multan was established in 2011 with a noble purpose of serving the region in the field of education. We made a modest beginning with the establishment of two departments, Department of Management Sciences and Department of Computer Science that run courses at the level of BS, Masters and MS Programs. In these two disciplines and many others in vision, our sole commitment is to impart quality education to students in an environment conducive to learning from both theoretical and practical dimensions. Proudly, the determined and highly qualified faculty integrates these objectives with the social ones to prepare students both qualitatively and quantitatively.
